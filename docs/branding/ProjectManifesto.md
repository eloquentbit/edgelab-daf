## Why We Started

This project was not born from the idea of creating another trading platform.

It started with a simple problem.

Every trading strategy generated data, but turning that data into reliable knowledge required building custom tools, writing analysis scripts and repeatedly solving the same engineering problems.

Instead of asking how to analyze trades better, we started asking a different question:

**Why isn't there a common foundation for quantitative trading research?**

The answer to that question became the EdgeLab ecosystem.

## Mission

**Transform trading data into knowledge.**

Everything we build exists for one purpose:

To help traders, developers and researchers transform trading facts into reliable, meaningful knowledge.

We believe that better decisions come from better understanding, and better understanding starts with trustworthy data.

## Vision

We envision an ecosystem where quantitative trading research is built upon canonical, reliable and reproducible datasets.

By separating data acquisition from data analysis, we aim to establish a common foundation that enables better tools, better research and ultimately better understanding of trading systems.

We believe that quantitative research should not be limited to those with advanced data engineering skills.


By lowering the technical barriers between trading and data analysis, we aim to make rigorous quantitative research accessible to a wider community of traders, developers and researchers.

We believe that preserving factual trading information today enables better analysis, better research and better decisions tomorrow.

We believe that better tools should amplify human understanding, not replace it.

## Values

### Truth Before Opinion

Reliable knowledge starts with reliable facts.

We record what happened before trying to explain why it happened.

---

### Engineering Before Convenience

We believe that investing time in architecture produces software that remains maintainable over time.

Shortcuts are easy.

Good engineering is intentional.

---

### Evidence Over Assumptions

Ideas are valuable.

Evidence is essential.

Every hypothesis should be testable.

---

### Simplicity Through Design

Complexity should never be transferred to users.

Our responsibility is to absorb complexity and expose simple, coherent tools.

---

### Open Knowledge

Knowledge grows when it can be shared, discussed and improved.

We believe open standards benefit the entire quantitative trading community.

---

### Curiosity

Every improvement starts with a question.

We believe that asking better questions is the first step toward better understanding.

## What We Don't Build

We do not build systems that promise profitable trading.

We do not build black boxes.

We do not build tools that replace critical thinking.

Our goal is different.

We build tools that help traders understand their own trading activity and make better informed decisions.

## Long-Term Vision

We envision an open ecosystem where data acquisition, dataset standards and quantitative analysis are independent but seamlessly connected.

Our ambition is not to build every tool.

Our ambition is to establish a foundation upon which better tools can be built.

Our success will not be measured by the number of tools we build, but by the quality of knowledge they enable.
